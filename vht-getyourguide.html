Who We Are

VHT – Vietnam Holiday Tours is a premier destination management company specializing in creating extraordinary travel experiences across Southeast Asia.

Based in the heart of Vietnam, VHT has spent over 15 years crafting unique, comprehensive, all-inclusive travel experiences that showcase the very best of Southeast Asia. We operate across Vietnam, Cambodia, Laos, and Thailand — four countries rich in culture, history, natural beauty, and adventure.

What sets VHT apart is our ability to deliver truly tailored experiences at every scale. Whether it's a large enterprise group of 200+ guests, a corporate incentive trip, a small private family getaway, or an intimate honeymoon, we design every itinerary from scratch with meticulous attention to detail.

Our deep local knowledge, trusted supplier relationships, and passionate team of travel designers ensure that every journey is seamless, authentic, and unforgettable. We don't offer off-the-shelf packages — we create stories.

Where We Operate

Four Extraordinary Countries, One Seamless Experience

We craft cross-border itineraries that flow effortlessly between countries, giving travelers the richest possible Southeast Asian experience.

Vietnam

From the terraced rice fields of Sapa to the emerald waters of Ha Long Bay, the ancient streets of Hoi An, the vibrant energy of Ho Chi Minh City, and the serene Mekong Delta — Vietnam is a journey through contrasts.

North to South
Cambodia

Explore the awe-inspiring temples of Angkor Wat, cruise along Tonle Sap Lake, discover Phnom Penh's resilient spirit, and unwind on the pristine beaches of Sihanoukville and Koh Rong.

Temples & Culture
Laos

The gentle soul of Southeast Asia — experience the tranquil charm of Luang Prabang, cruise the Mekong, visit sacred caves, explore the mysterious Plain of Jars, and witness almsgiving at dawn.

Hidden Gem
Thailand

From Bangkok's dazzling temples and legendary street food to Chiang Mai's mountain culture, the paradise islands of the south, and the historic ruins of Ayutthaya — Thailand dazzles at every turn.
